Why I Love Pop Music

Pop music has always been one of my biggest passions. I love how there's always a song for every situation you go through. I love hearing all the different rythms and creativty from music artists from all the different genres. The way they're able to express their feelings through their music writing is truly a great experience. I love how music can bring everyone together even when we come from different countires and speak different languages. Music can be really powerful if it is made with passion!

Why These Artists Inspire Me

Michael Jackson is one of the greatest performers of all time and changed pop music forever way before we were ready. The amount of excitment that he would bring to the stage and to people's ears is something so powerful, and even years later he still makes a big impact on every generations. Bruno Mars is one of the most talked about artists today and his way of expressing his feeling through his music is what connnects him to his fans. Prince are also amazing performers who bring energy and talent to every stage. He was known to be Michael Jackson's rival and they're way of pushing each other is what made the music industry what it is today.

Taylor Swift stands out because of her storytelling and songwriting. Harry Styles brings confidence and individuality to his music. The Weeknd has a unique sound that makes his music unforgettable.

Kali Uchis and Bad Bunny bring creativity and cultural influence into pop music, making the genre even more diverse and exciting.
